About

We created Darg with the goal of abolishing status meetings once and for all.

Darg aims to provide a beautiful and seamless communication tool so that our users can focus on what's truly important to them.

Our technology stack is what we jokingly refer to as the "Angre" stack - Angular.js on the frontend, and Clojure on the backend. We are hosted on AWS, and use Postgres as our data storage engine.

Darg is an UrsaCorp project.

Stay Up To Date The Easy Way

We help you know what your team is up to without wasting their time. Whether it's through email or automatic feeds, we make it easy for everybody to be on the same page.

How It Works

Every day, we send you a short e-mail asking what you've done. All you have to do is reply.

We take your replies and use them to fill out a log of your activity that day. You can also enter your tasks and achievements via our web UI. At the end of the day, we send out a summary email with everybody's activity for the day.

At a glance, see what your friends and teammates have been up to!

Hate Writing Things Down?

We've got you covered.

Your tools already track your activity - why write down what they already know? We're working on easily configurable integrations with GitHub and Trello that tie into your existing workflow.

Keep everyone informed - without the extra work.

Pricing

Darg is completely free for teams of 3 or fewer. After that, it's $5 per user per month.

We believe that simple pricing plans are best, and that's what we've done with Darg. No hidden clauses or catches - just a free tier for small teams, and a flat rate for everyone after that.



Enterprise
