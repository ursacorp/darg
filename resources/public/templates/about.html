About

We created Darg with the goal of abolishing status meetings once and for all.

Darg aims to provide a beautiful and seamless communication tool so that our users can focus on what's truly important to them.

Our technology stack is what we jokingly refer to as the "Angre" stack - Angular.js on the frontend, and Clojure on the backend. We are hosted on AWS, and use Postgres as our data storage engine.

Darg is an UrsaCorp project.
