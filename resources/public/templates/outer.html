How It Works

Every day, we send you a short e-mail asking what you've done. All you have to do is reply.

We take your replies and use them to fill out a log of your activity that day. You can also enter your tasks and achievements via our web UI. At the end of the day, we send out a summary email with everybody's activity for the day.
