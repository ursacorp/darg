Get automatic reports from the tools you already use.

How It Works

Every day, we send you a short e-mail asking what you did that day - all you have to do is reply. At the end of the day, your team's responses are batched up and emailed out to the entire team.

You can also enter tasks as you complete them via our web UI.

Don't want to reply to an e-mail? No problem! We're working on easily configurable integrations with GitHub and Trello that tie into your existing workflow.
