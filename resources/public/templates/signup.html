Darg

/dɑːɡ/ noun

1. Scot. and North English. a day's work

2. Australian. a fixed or definite amount of work; a work quota.

Say goodbye to status meetings.

With Darg.io, we send you a daily e-mail asking what you did that day - all you have to do is reply, and your Darg is rolled up and sent to everybody on your team.

Don't want to reply to an e-mail? No problem! We've got easy to set up integrations with GitHub and Trello that tie into your existing workflow.
